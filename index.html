About

We are Brown University's First Financial/Technology Hackathon! As part of Brown Financial Club, we have created an event where people interested in financial technology, blockchain, and much more can come together and create new, innovative hacks.

Information

The event will likely occur in November 2018. We have still not finalized our venue, but we will be located within or nearby Brown's Campus. Students from all areas are welcome!
